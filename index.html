What is OOPADAI!?

Oopadai is a "curated web" online network that connects users by telephone with expert mentors. User can seek advice on varying topics ranging from business development to career advice, startup advice or advice related to legal matters etc. We have experienced mentors to guide you at each stage in any field. Advice is given in the form of 1-to-1 calls between the user and the expert mentor.
